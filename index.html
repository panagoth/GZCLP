Workout Schedule

Day 1

  • Squat: 3 sets of 5 reps (T1)
  • Bench Press: 3 sets of 5 reps (T1)
  • Rows: 3 sets of 10 reps (T2)
  • Accessory Work: Core exercises, 3 sets of 12-15 reps (T3)

Day 2

  • Deadlift: 1 set of 5 reps (T1)
  • Overhead Press: 3 sets of 5 reps (T1)
  • Pull-ups: 3 sets of 10 reps (T2)
  • Accessory Work: Arm exercises, 3 sets of 12-15 reps (T3)

Day 3

  • Squat (lighter weight): 3 sets of 5 reps (T1)
  • Bench Press (lighter weight): 3 sets of 5 reps (T1)
  • Rows: 3 sets of 10 reps (T2)
  • Accessory Work: Leg exercises, 3 sets of 12-15 reps (T3)

Notes:

  • T1: Primary lifts, heavier weights.
  • T2: Secondary lifts, moderate weights.
  • T3: Accessory work, lighter weights.
  • Progression: Increase weights gradually each week, focusing on form and technique.
  • Warm-up: Include dynamic stretching and lighter sets before each main lift.
